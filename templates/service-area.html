Built for Here

Over-engineered for Florida's Heat, Humidity & Hurricane Season.

Florida Chicken Coops builds premium backyard chicken coops specifically engineered for Florida's climate — from the humidity and salt air of the coast to the intense summer heat and hurricane exposure found across the state. Every coop is constructed using ground-contact-rated lumber, corrosion-resistant hardware, hurricane connectors, and galvanized-after-welding mesh designed to withstand Florida weather long-term.

Most backyard coops are designed for mild climates and shipped nationwide. Ours are built in Florida, by people who understand what Florida weather does to outdoor structures over time.

We deliver fully assembled coops directly to your property, anchor them securely in place, and walk you through every feature before we leave.
