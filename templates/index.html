Florida is one of the hardest environments in the country to build an outdoor structure that lasts. Sustained heat. Coastal humidity. Termites. Wind-driven rain. Hurricane-force gusts. Predators that have evolved alongside backyard flocks for generations.

Most chicken coops on the market are built for appearance. Ours are engineered for the reality of keeping a flock alive and thriving through a Florida summer.
